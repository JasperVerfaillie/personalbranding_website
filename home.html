About me

Hi, thanks for visiting my webpage. My name is Jasper Verfaillie and I used to be a journalism and graphic design student. Now I'm branching out into the world, trying to put my skills to the test. This is a brief overview of my work, both design stuff and journalism stuff. If you're intrigued by what you see, click on the card to discover more about it. Because why tell you all about how I can work with Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, Avid, Reaper ... if I can just show it to you. Cut to the chase. Show, don't tell, right?
